Character Profile: Basilio

Basic Information

  • Name: Basilio
  • Age: Approximately 10 years old at the beginning of Noli Me Tangere

Background

Educational Background: Eager student facing hardships; seeks knowledge despite limited education, reflecting the struggles of Filipino youth during Spanish colonization.

Characteristics

  • Personality Traits: Innocent, determined, resilient, observant, and caring.
  • Strengths: Unwavering determination, resilience in adversity, commitment to education, empathetic and caring nature.
  • Weakness: Youth and inexperience limit his influence, making him vulnerable to the harsh realities of colonial society.

Key Moments

Key moments for Basilio include his poignant interactions with his mother, Sisa, showcasing his deep love and concern for her wellbeing. His experiences also include witnessing the injustices of colonial rule and the suffering of his fellow Filipinos, which shapes his understanding of society. Additionally, his involvement in the tragic events surrounding the deaths of Sisa and other characters highlights the impact of oppression on innocent lives.

End Status

By the end of the novel, Basilio becomes an orphan and is left to navigate a harsh and unforgiving world. His journey reflects the broader struggle of the Filipino people during this tumultuous period.

Symbolism

Basilio symbolizes the hope and potential of the Filipino youth during the Spanish colonial era. His character represents the innocence of childhood amidst the chaos of oppression and the desire for education as a means of liberation.
